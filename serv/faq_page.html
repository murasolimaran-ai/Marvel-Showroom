💰 What EMI options are available?
▾

We offer flexible EMI plans starting from ₹8,000/month with tenures of 12 to 84 months. Zero-cost EMI options are also available on select models in partnership with leading banks. Call us to get a personalized quote.

🚚 How long does delivery take?
▾

For in-stock vehicles, delivery typically takes 3–5 working days after documentation is complete. For custom or pre-booked orders, it may take 4–8 weeks depending on the manufacturer. We offer doorstep delivery within 15 km.

🛡️ What warranty is provided on vehicles?
▾

New vehicles come with the manufacturer's standard warranty of 3 to 5 years or 1,00,000 km (whichever comes first). Certified Pre-Owned vehicles carry a Marvel 1-year comprehensive warranty. Extended warranty packages are also available.

📄 What documents are required to purchase?
▾

You'll need: (1) Government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar/Passport/Driving Licence), (2) Address proof, (3) PAN card, (4) 3–6 months bank statements for finance applications, (5) Passport-size photos. Our team will guide you through the entire process.

🏎️ Can I book a test drive?
▾

Absolutely! Test drives are free of charge. You can book online through our website, call us at +91 98745 63210, or simply walk into the showroom Mon–Sat between 9 AM and 7 PM. A valid driving licence is required.

🏦 Do you offer vehicle financing / loans?
▾

Yes! We work with 10+ leading banks and NBFCs including HDFC, ICICI, SBI, and Kotak to offer competitive loan rates. We help with the entire application process — approvals in as little as 24 hours with minimal paperwork.

🔧 Is after-sales service available?
▾

Yes, Marvel Showroom provides full after-sales support including scheduled maintenance, accidental repair, AC service, engine diagnostics, and roadside assistance. Our ASE-certified technicians are available Mon–Sat.

🔄 Can I trade in / exchange my old car?
▾

Yes! We offer hassle-free trade-in valuations. Bring your old vehicle to our showroom for a free assessment. The valuation is applied directly as a discount on your new purchase. We accept all makes and models.

🚗 Is free pickup & drop available for servicing?
▾

Yes! We offer complimentary pickup and drop for all service appointments within a 15 km radius of our showroom. Simply schedule your service online or call us 24 hours in advance to arrange pickup.
