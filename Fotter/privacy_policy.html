Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information at Marvel Showroom.

Last Updated January 2026
01 — INTRODUCTION

Your Privacy Matters

At Marvel Showroom, we are fully committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

By using our website or services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

02 — DATA WE COLLECT

Information We Collect

  • Full name, email address, and phone number when you contact us or book a test drive
  • Browsing behavior and page interaction data for improving our website
  • Device type, browser version, and IP address for security and analytics
  • Preferences and wishlist selections when you interact with our car models
03 — HOW WE USE IT

Purpose of Data Usage

We use your information solely to provide and improve our services — including responding to inquiries, scheduling test drives, sending relevant vehicle offers, and enhancing your website experience.

🔒 Your data is never sold to third parties. We share it only with trusted service partners (e.g., payment processors, delivery partners) bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
04 — YOUR RIGHTS

Control Over Your Data

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete your information at any time
  • Opt out of marketing communications with a single click
  • Request data portability in a structured format
