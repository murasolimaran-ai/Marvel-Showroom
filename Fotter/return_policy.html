Return Policy

Clear and transparent return and refund guidelines for all Marvel Showroom purchases.

Last Updated January 2026
01 — RETURN WINDOW

Eligibility Period

Marvel Showroom accepts return requests within 7 days of the purchase date. Beyond this window, returns may not be accepted unless the vehicle has a documented manufacturer defect or warranty issue.

📅 The return window begins from the date of the Purchase Agreement signing, not delivery date. Please plan accordingly.
02 — CONDITIONS

Return Conditions

  • Vehicle must be returned in its original, unmodified condition
  • Mileage must not exceed 500 km from delivery odometer reading
  • All original documents, keys, and accessories must be included
  • No physical damage, scratches, or alterations should be present
  • The vehicle must not have been in any incident or insurance claim
03 — REFUND PROCESS

How Refunds Work

Once the returned vehicle passes our inspection, we will initiate your refund. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days directly to your original payment method or as a bank transfer.

Processing fees, registration charges, and any applicable road taxes are non-refundable as per government regulations.

04 — NON-RETURNABLE

Non-Returnable Situations

  • Vehicles purchased under special discount or clearance sale events
  • Custom-ordered or pre-booked vehicles with confirmed specifications
  • Vehicles that have been registered in the buyer's name
  • Returns requested after the 7-day window without prior written approval
05 — HOW TO RETURN

Return Process

  • Contact our team via phone or email within the 7-day window
  • Our representative will schedule a vehicle inspection at our showroom
  • Submit original purchase documents and keys at the showroom
  • Refund will be initiated post successful inspection clearance
