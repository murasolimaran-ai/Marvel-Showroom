Cookie Policy

Understand how Marvel Showroom uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience.

Last Updated January 2026
01 — WHAT ARE COOKIES

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, improve performance, and provide personalized experiences. Cookies do not contain personal identifying information by themselves.

02 — COOKIES WE USE

Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies — Required for the website to function correctly (navigation, security)
  • Performance Cookies — Collect anonymous data on how visitors use our site to help us improve
  • Preference Cookies — Remember your settings like language or region for a smoother experience
  • Analytics Cookies — Help us understand traffic patterns and popular content via tools like Google Analytics
03 — THIRD-PARTY COOKIES

External Services

Some cookies on our site are placed by third-party services such as Google, Facebook, and embedded map providers. These services have their own privacy and cookie policies, which we encourage you to review.

🍪 We do not use cookies to collect sensitive personal data or track you across other websites for advertising purposes.
04 — MANAGING COOKIES

Your Cookie Controls

You have full control over cookies. You can manage or disable them through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, Search & Services → Cookies
